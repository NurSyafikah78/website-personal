My Family

My family is quite big, we got nine (9) in total! All of us were born

in Kuala Terengganu, Terengganu.I have seven (7) siblings which consists of two (2) sisters and

four (4) brothers.As I already mentioned before, I’m the seventh out of seventh!


My parents were both from the year of 80’s and even if it sounds

cliché, they are the greatest parents and I’m proud to address them like that to people

who don’t even know them yet. The oldest one in my siblings is a woman, a strong one at that.

She’s thirty-three (33) years old and her name is Norhan. The second one is Fatihat

and she is thirty-two (32) years old. We love to call her as as our second mother,

because that is how important she is in our in our family. She might not know this,

but she is the sole reason why I even bother to do well on my studies. All of my family

play the biggest roles in my life, but I would like to name her as the most loveable.


All the next third till sixth siblings are guys. Started from the age of thirty (30)

years old, twenty-eight (28) years old, twenty-seven (27) years old, and twenty-three (23) years old.

Their names are actually quite easy to remember. Started from Wahid, Zulkarnain,

Firdaus and Amiruddin. Might have to thank my parents for giving us the simplest names

as it will be easy to keep their names in most of my prayers.


This big family have shared tons of amazing memories, even the sad one

I’m not the one who love to express myself, but wholeheartedly,

I love them. I might be young and reckless, but I put them

on my priority lists. There were none above them, and I’m glad

that I still have them all.
